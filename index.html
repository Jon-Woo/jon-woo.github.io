About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at UCLA studying mathematics. For undergrad, I also studied math at UCLA. As an undergraduate student, I was interested in differential equations and numerical analysis with applications in fluid dynamics. I am still exploring my interests as a graduate student.

I am also very passionate about teaching science and math. I have experience teaching and tutoring elementary, middle, and high school math as well as college level math as a teaching assistant. I am open to tutoring UCLA students (see Teaching page).
