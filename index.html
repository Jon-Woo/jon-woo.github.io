About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at UCLA studying applied mathematics. My academic interests generally include differential equations and numerical analysis with applications to science and engineering. I am also very passionate about teaching and tutoring students in science and math (see the Teaching page to learn more).

Outside of the classroom, you can usually find me playing basketball and tennis, reading, or occasionally on the video game.
